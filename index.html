About Me

The Engineer Who Thinks in Systems

I've been building software since 2012 — long before AI became a buzzword. What started as a passion for clean, pixel-perfect frontends evolved into a deep expertise across the full stack, from architecting React and Next.js applications to building Node.js APIs, CI/CD pipelines, and CMS-driven platforms that agencies and enterprises rely on daily.

Today, I'm at the intersection of software engineering and artificial intelligence. I don't just write code — I design systems. I help businesses identify where intelligent automation creates leverage, then build it: custom AI agents, n8n and Make.com workflows, LLM integrations with Claude and OpenAI, and agentic pipelines that handle entire business processes end-to-end.

Whether you're a startup needing an MVP, an agency wanting a technical partner, or an established business ready to automate — I bring strategic thinking, deep engineering skill, and genuine curiosity about what AI makes possible next.
